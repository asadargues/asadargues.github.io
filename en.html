Belgian Beer

packaging

  • Agency: Altavia Benelux (now AltaviaACT*)
  • Tools: Illustrator &/or Photoshop

Here are some designs research for a Belgian beer that's all about a local celebration in the city of Mons called the "Doudou". It's celebrating Saint Georges killing a green and red dragon to protect the inhabitants. "Chambourlette" is a specific term designating people who go and celebrate with the Montois, even if they're not from that region.The colors of the city are white and red.
